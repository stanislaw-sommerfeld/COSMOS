The path minimizes the true control energy ½‖u‖² with u = B·ξ + c₀ and B = [[D₂−3n²I , −2n·D₁] , [2n·D₁ , D₂]]. The descent is preconditioned by (BᵀB)⁻¹ — that is what makes "smoothing" mean "spend less fuel". At n → 0, B → blockdiag(D₂, D₂): standard CHOMP, and the optimum is the straight line.
Pure JavaScript port of cosmos.chomp_cw (2D, no rendezvous boundary term) — same operators as the Python mission, running live in your browser. Δv is the L1 control integral under the true CW dynamics; the free-space baseline plans at n=0 and pays under the current n.
